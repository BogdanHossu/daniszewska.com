Music lessons booking app

About

MusicTutors is an online platform for booking music lessons. It connects students of any age with highly-professional tutors. The platform offers music lessons that take place in person or in an online classroom.

Concept

The app was designed for teachers to enable booking and re-scheduling music lessons from the phone. The modern and easy-to-use interface makes it possible to keep an eye on the agenda, plan new lessons, chat with students and require payments.

My Role

I was the only designer working on this project. I aimed at creating a user-friendly and easy-to-use interface while deciding on project direction. I was responsible for concept development, wireframing and end-design.
