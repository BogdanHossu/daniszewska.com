MusicTutors - new booking system

About

MusicTutors is an online platform for booking music lessons. It connects students of any age with highly-professional tutors. The platform offers music lessons that take place in person or in an online classroom.

Concept

The project consisted of re-design of MusicTutors platform, both public and logged-in parts of the page. The focus lied in creating aesthetically pleasing and easy-to-navigate music lessons booking site.

My Role

During the process of the platform re-design, I focused on understanding current issues and communication improvements. I worked as an only designer on the project, and I was responsible for defining an initial concept and providing the final solution.
