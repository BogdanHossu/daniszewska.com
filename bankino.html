About

Bankino is an international, online platform that helps users to compare different loan offers. Currently, it is available in Denmark, Finland, Spain and the Czech Republic.

Concept

This platform aimed to provide users with easy-to-use and transparent loan comparison. The customers can have a quick look at the essential information about the offer but also unfold and read more details. What is more, the communication about the loan process and the possibilities are well explained.

My Role

Worked as a designer, contributed to the concept and wireframing process, created a brand identity and brand manual, designed all of the website screens.
