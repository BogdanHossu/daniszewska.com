About

Harmo – simply feel, is the mobile app concept that was created in order to give people the opportunity to be aware of their feelings and find a way to prevent long periods of negative feelings.This project was my response to the design challenge raised by The Stanford Center on Longevity. It was focused on ideas that create and support healthy habits - including financial, physical, and social behaviours.

Concept

The app was designed to encourage users to share their emotions introspectively and reflect daily on feelings. Through a series of simple questions about daily activities, user can share both positive and negative emotions. Harold - app character, was created to support the users and make them smile.

My Role

I worked on each part of the concept development process, from research and wireframing to user-testing and final design project. What is more, I was also responsible for project planning, organisation and management.
